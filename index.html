About Shrey Mallick

A Young Leader with a Vision

Shrey Mallick (Hindi: श्रेय मल्लिक) is a dynamic young leader born on August 20, 2000. He is an Indian student activist, former student of Uttarakhand Technical University, and a dedicated member of the Bharatiya Janata Party.

Born into a political family in Patna, Bihar, Shrey has been exposed to the intricacies of public service from an early age. His father is a well-known politician in India, providing Shrey with valuable insights into governance and public administration.

As a member of Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha, the youth wing of BJP, Shrey is committed to bringing fresh perspectives and innovative solutions to the challenges facing our nation. His dedication to student activism and youth empowerment makes him a voice for the new generation of India.
